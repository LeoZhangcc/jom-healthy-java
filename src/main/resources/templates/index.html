Why JomHealthy?

Malaysian families with children aged 7-12 are facing significant challenges with nutrition. With busy schedules and the prevalence of unhealthy snacks and fast food, it's hard to ensure your child gets the balanced diet they need for proper growth and development.

JomHealthy bridges the gap between complex nutritional data and everyday food choices. We provide accessible, trustworthy, and practical nutrition support for parents while making healthy eating fun and engaging for kids.

Understand your child's current nutritional and physical health
Translate complex health data into practical daily food decisions
Make learning about healthy foods fun and engaging for children
Overcome language barriers with full trilingual support

App Features

Everything You Need for a Healthy Child

JomHealthy is packed with intuitive tools designed specifically for parents and children to build lasting, healthy habits together.

Social Health Insights

Access reliable health statistics based on MOH & WHO data, paired with quick daily nutrition tips.

Body Condition Checker

Easily track your child's BMI and physical condition with color-coded results and actionable suggestions.

AI Smart Meal Assistant

Generate healthy recipes, automatically create shopping lists, and navigate to nearby grocery stores.

Kid-Friendly Explanations

Translate complex nutritional data into visual, relatable concepts like sugar cubes and color indicators.

Interactive Pet System

A fun digital companion that reacts to food choices, making health education interactive for your child.

Growth Milestone Analytics

Track and visualize height and weight data over time, with alerts for abnormal growth patterns.

Trilingual Support

Seamlessly switch between English, Bahasa Melayu, and Chinese to suit your family's preferences.

AI Voice Companion

Log meals quickly using voice commands and receive simple, instant AI-generated nutrition feedback.

Designed for Parents, Loved by Kids

Experience a smooth, intuitive interface that makes tracking growth, generating meal plans, and learning about nutrition feel effortless and fun.
